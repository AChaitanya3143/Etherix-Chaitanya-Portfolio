Frequently Asked Questions
Do you require full payment upfront?
No. I work on a 50/50 model — 50% at kickoff, 50% on final delivery. For larger projects, milestone-based payments can be arranged.
What if I need something between two plans?
Every project is scoped to your exact needs. Use the Custom Quote option below to describe your requirements and I'll build a bespoke package.
Do I own the code after delivery?
Absolutely. Full source code ownership transfers to you on final payment. No licensing fees, no hidden hooks, no dependencies on me to keep your site running.
How long does a project actually take?
Starter: 3–5 days. Growth: 7–10 days. Elite: 14–21 days. Commerce: 21–30 days. Timelines depend on content readiness and revision rounds.
Can you handle hosting and domain too?
Yes. I can guide you through purchasing hosting and a domain, or manage the entire deployment for you at no extra cost with most plans.
What's included in the free support period?
Bug fixes, minor text/image updates, performance issues, and compatibility issues. Major new features are billed separately at add-on rates.

Got Something Bigger in Mind?

SaaS product, complex web app, or enterprise portal? Let's scope it out. Every large project gets a tailored quote and a dedicated project roadmap.
